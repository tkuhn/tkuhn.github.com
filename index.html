Welcome to my website. I am a tenured assistant professor at VU University Amsterdam and starting entrepreneur focussing on how we can apply semantic technologies in a user-friendly manner to radically improve the communication of knowledge.

More on Orcid, Google Scholar, arXiv, LinkedIn, GitHub, and Twitter.

My Topics and Projects

  • My new venture to build the ecosystem of semantic-born knowledge: Knowledge Pixels
  • Small semantic packages called nanopublications can be published through a decentralized network, based on trusty URIs and the nanopub-java library
  • Nanodash (code) is a web tool to browse and publish nanopublications, based on user-friendly templates
  • Controlled natural languages like ACE allow for formal yet fully intuitive knowledge representation, for example in the form of the semantic wiki AceWiki
  • The FAIR Principles aim to make research data machine-understandable across disciplines, for example in the social sciences through ODISSEI
  • Past projects: FAIR is as FAIR does, Capture Bias, ControCurator, Attempto, MOLTO, ERGO, REWERSE
